THE BEST RECIPE FOR CREAMY GARLIC PARMESAN PASTA

Garlic Parmesan pasta is a rich and creamy dish (usually spaghetti or fettuccine) tossed in a velvet garlic sauce butter with a generous amount of Parmesan cheese that melts into the sauce.

Here's a simple Garlic Parmesan Pasta recipe

Ingredients:

  • 300g spaghetti
  • 3 tbsp butter
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 4 cloves garlic,minced
  • salt and pepper
Optional Add-ins
  • Grilled chicken breast, sliced
  • Sauteed shrimp
  • Steamed broccoli florets
  • Sun-kissed tomatoes

Steps/Instructions:

  1. Cook the pasta according to the package directions in a large pot of salted water until al dente. reserve 1/2 cup of pasta water before draining.
  2. While pasta is cooking, melt butter in a large skillet over medium heat. Add minced garlic and saute for1-2mins until fragrant but not brown.
  3. Pour in the heavy cream, simmer for 3-4mins, stirring occationally.
  4. Reduce heat to low and gradually whisk in the grated Parmesan cheese until sauce is smooth and creamy, season with salt and pepper
  5. Toss cooked spaghetti in the sauce. cook for 1-2mins until heated through.
  6. Garnish with parsley and extra Parmesan cheese before serving.

Chef's tips

  • Use freshly grated Parmesan:Pre-shredded cheese contains anti-caking agents that can prevent it from melting smoothly
  • Don't over cook the garlic:Garlic burns easily and becomes bitter. Cook just until fragnant
  • Reserve pasta water:The starchy pasta water helps create a silky, emulsified sauce that clings to the pasta
  • Serve immediately:Cream-based sauces tend to thicken as they cool, so serve this dish right away for the best texture.
